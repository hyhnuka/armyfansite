Map of The Soul 7
Story of self-discovery, tracing the honest and heartfelt journey of BTS as they navigate the complexities of their true selves. The tale begins with MAP OF THE SOUL: PERSONA, where BTS explores the joy and exhilaration of love, and the ways in which they present themselves to the world. This chapter is filled with a sense of wonder as they come to understand the self they reveal to others, the version that exists in the gaze of those around them.

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Pt.2
About capturing the unyielding energy of young people who forge ahead, embracing the unknown despite their fears. Now starting to feel the bitterness of adulthood, they begin to realize that life is not always beautiful. However, they continued to run forward, driven only by the fire in their hearts and their tired legs. There is no choice but to keep moving, even when faced with the struggles of youth. They stumble, they fall, but they still shout to the world that "it's okay to fall, it's okay to get hurt." After all, this is the true beauty of the moment.

You Never Walk Alone
A profound message of hope and solidarity, reminding us that we do not have to face life's difficulties alone. It reflects the comfort and support that can be found in those around us, even in our darkest times. This album shows that although pain, loss and struggle are an inevitable part of life, we find strength when we live it together with the people we love. It acknowledges the loneliness and gravity of difficult times but emphasizes that, with the presence of others, the burden becomes a little lighter.

MAP of The Soul 7

MAP OF THE SOUL series contains the honest and heartfelt story of BTS and their journey of discovering their true selves. The first chapter of this story, “MAP OF THE SOUL : PERSONA”, sang about the world and the joy of love, and the discovery of the self as it is displayed to the world; “MAP OF THE SOUL : 7” now turns inward to the shadow within in a candid acknowledgement that it, too, is part of the self.
